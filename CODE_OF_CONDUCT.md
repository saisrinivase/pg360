# Code of Conduct

PG360 aims to be a practical, respectful project for DBAs, developers, and contributors from different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Give technical feedback with evidence and curiosity.
- Welcome questions from people who are still learning.
- Keep disagreements focused on the work, not the person.
- Protect sensitive operational information when sharing examples.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, ridicule, or repeated bad-faith argument.
- Sharing private credentials, customer data, or confidential infrastructure details without permission.
- Turning technical disagreement into gatekeeping or hostility.

## Maintainer responsibility

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, remove harmful content, or restrict participation when behavior makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, release notes, and other community spaces tied to PG360.
